Connect Four is a two player online game. The game board consist of a 6-row by 7-column grid.

Rules of the game:

  • It is a two player game where each player has a set of colored tokens (usually Red and Yellow).
  • On each turn, a player drops one of their tokens from the top into any of the columns.
  • The goal is to be the first to get four of your tokens in a rowâ€”either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  • The game ends immediately when a player achieves this.
  • If the board fills up completely without any player achieving four in a row, the game is considered a draw.
